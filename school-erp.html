Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

School ERP: All You Need to Know

  • What is the ERP system in schools?

    ERP stands for Enterprise Resource Planning system. It is software designed to handle hassle-free academic and administrative functions of daily school activity. If you want a school ERP for your school, contact us.

  • What is the cost of school ERP software?

    The cost of school ERP software varies according to customer needs and the ERP features required.

  • What are the 5 components of ERP?

    The 5 components of ERP are finance, human resources, supply chain management, customer relationship, and manufacturing and production.

  • What is the difference between ERP and SIS?

    SIS focuses on student-related tasks from registration to graduation, while ERP handles broader institutional operations such as budgeting, payroll, and more.
