Parent Mobile App

A parent mobile app normally refers to an application that helps parents to manage various aspects of their children’s lives. It means their education and online activity. A parent mobile application can range from school specific apps that provide only academic progress and school events update to broader parental control apps that manage screen time and online safety. You can choose anyone of them.


Here, we give a detailed breakdown about parents mobile applications, have a look.

School Specific Parent App

The purpose of school specific parent app is to facilitate communicate and information sharing between schools and parents.

Features of Parent Mobile Apps

The features of a parent mobile application is given below please check.

  • With parent mobile app, they can access to academic information of children like grades, attendance and homework that assign to the children.
  • Using this app, you can also get notification about school events, announcements and important dates.
  • It is a direct communication channel with teacher and school administration. Some of the best parent mobile apps are Apeejay Parent app, School Plus App and Parent Eye. If you also want a custom parent app then contact us. We will develop that for you at most affordable price.

Parental Control App

The purpose of this app is monitor and manage children’s digital activity and online safety.

  • Screen time management: You can set time limit on device usage and specific app access.
  • Web flitering and content monitoring: Using this app, you can block the access of inappropriate websites and content.
  • Track location: This feature allowing parents to see their child’s location.
  • Social Medic Monitoring: Nowadays, there are so many frauds happen through social media so it is very necessary to monitor social media monitoring.
